About me

Hi all,My name is Petar Nikolov. I'm from Bulgaria. I have been living in United Kingdom for last 4 years. Till one year ago, I was thinking "Just 6 months more and I go back in my country", and like that they past 4, almost 5 years. One year ago happen some thing's in my life who make me think - what I want, what I do for it and why. The job in the warehouse is not bad, but money - time - stress is not fair. I want better future. This is the main reason to apply for Full stack developer Bootcamp in CodeYourFuture. The reason for me to wish I to become professional developer is exactly in this two words. I want to be professional in something what I like, something what I can work no matter which country I live. This is job with alot of benefits. Is not so much about the money, how much is important for me the freedom and the feeling you create something useful. And the third questions " How much effort are you willing to put in during the course?" Everyone who apply for this course should know how much is important and how many opportunity will have everyone person who past the course successfully. The best way is to work hard, the results will come after that. Before I apply for this course I see many people who are without experience in IT or even similar. I don't have any background experience, but I'm ready to learn and put myself 100% in this course. My English is definitely not perfect and this will be good opportunity for me to improve it. In my past experience in UK I have been working only hard work in warehouses. The reality is if you not working overtime you not saving anything. I was looking for something like this from long time and now I find even better, this course from Code our Future.
